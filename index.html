FAQ

  • How often are new updates released?

    Updates with new worlds, events, and features are released regularly.

  • How do I start playing?

    Download the game from Google Play, complete the tutorial, and start spinning the slots to earn coins and build your city.

  • What devices are supported?

    Coin Gangster is available on Android devices.

  • Can I play offline?

    Some features require an internet connection, but certain elements are accessible offline.

  • Are there in-app purchases?

    Yes, optional purchases are available for boosters, spins, and special rewards.

  • What are the cards used for?

    Cards unlock rewards, accelerate progress, and enhance gameplay. Complete card sets to earn exclusive prizes.

  • How do I protect my city from raids?

    Use shields to defend your city and rebuild quickly after an attack.
